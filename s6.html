SITE 6 : Flipline Studio

https://www.flipline.com/

About the site

The Flipline Studios website serves as the official home for all things Papa Louie and related games. It's where players can access their favorite free-to-play browser games (e.g. Papa’s Pizzeria, Cupcakeria), learn about apps and updates, check the blog, and dive into fan community features.

About the author

Flipline Studios (also known as Flipline IDS LLC), an independent game development studio founded in 2004 in Cleveland, Ohio by Matt Neff and Tony Solary.

What I like the most

It’s the classic hub where I first played iconic Papa’s Gamerias for free in your browser—Papa’s Pizzeria, Burgeria, Cupcakeria, Freezeria, and more.

  • Nostalgic Flash Game Portal
  • Bright colors, playful typography, and icons of Papa characters—instantly recognizable and perfectly aligned with the games' cheery, cartoon look.
