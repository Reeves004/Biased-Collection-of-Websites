SITE 10 : THE INTERNET ARCHIVE

https://archive.org

About the site

he Internet Archive is a nonprofit digital library that preserves and provides free access to billions of webpages, books, movies, audio, software, and more.

About the author

Founded by Brewster Kahle

  • Founded: 1996
  • Mission: “Universal access to all knowledge”

What I like the most

Colors and theme and seeing the magic go.

  • It's one of the few places that actually cares about preservation.
  • It’s old-school but charmingly utilitarian.
  • Super minimal—looks like the early 2000s but feels timeless.
  • Function over form: search bar, categories, boom
