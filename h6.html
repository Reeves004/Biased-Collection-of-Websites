LOATHED LINK 6: THE USELESS WEB

https://www.merriam-webster.com

About the site

Merriam-Webster is a famous American company that publishes dictionaries, thesauruses, and other reference books in English. It's one of the most trusted sources for definitions, pronunciation, word usage, grammar help, and more.

  • Cluttered, ad-heavy home
  • Assault of features under definitions

About the author

Founded: 1831 by George and Charles Merriam; acquired Noah Webster’s legacy in 1843. Current owner: Encyclopædia Britannica, Inc., publisher of authoritative English reference works.

What I hate the most

The layout bombards users with a jumble of quizzes, trending words, blogs, ads, and subscription prompts. There's no clear hierarchy,making you hunt around just to find a word definition.
