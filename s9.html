SITE 9 : FALLINGFALLING

https:// fallingfalling.com/

About the site

fallingfalling.com (aka “Falling Falling”), a minimalist but hypnotic art‑website worth knowing.

About the author

Falling Falling .com is a minimalist web art piece created by Rafaël Rozendaal in 2011, with code by Reinier Feijen, and sound by Gloumouth1, in collaboration with collector Hampus Lindwall

What I like the most

Ultra‑minimalist UI: no distractions, no navigation, just the falling visuals.

  • Feels like a meditation of motion
  • Browser‑native performance—leverages canvas and web audio, no heavy frameworks.
