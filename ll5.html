LOATHED LINK 5 : SM ENT.

https://www.smentertainment.com/en/

About the site

SM’s sites lean heavily into visual overload — high-gloss animations, auto-playing videos, dynamic scrolls — but it’s often at the cost of navigation clarity. You sometimes feel like you're wandering through an Instagram ad for a fashion brand rather than getting actual info..

About the author

The SM Entertainment website serves as the official hub for SM and its SMTOWN projects, backed by the legacy of founder Lee Soo‑man.

What I hate the most the most

Accessibility is Almost Nonexistent No dark mode. No text resizing. Minimal alt text or ARIA labels for screen readers. That makes it a nightmare for visually impaired users, or even just people using older browsers.
